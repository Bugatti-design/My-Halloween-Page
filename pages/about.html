Legend has it that this house is haunted by spirits of the past...

On the edge of town stood the old mansion, known to locals as "The Whispering House." Though no one dared stay long, those who did said the house breathed, a deep, hollow sigh that seemed to come from the very walls. And if you stood still, just for a moment, you could hear a voice on the wind, a soft whisper calling, “Come closer…”
